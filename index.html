Work

Tereos

Middle Office Manager/Business Analyst/Full Project Holder• Feb 2013 - Nov 2016

Tereos is a global food industry that transforms raw materials into sugar, alcohol and starch. I worked in the raw material purchasing department in the grain business unit which covers 9 plants in 8 countries in Europe.

As Middle Office Manager, my head job. I was hire to create the MO activity and then to make it sustainable. A good definition of my role was to oversee the smooth running of the department. The Front Office are grain purchasers/traders, the Back Office are quality and administratif workers. My tasks are between, to figure out that all the processes inside each activities are up to date, the tools/softwares are 100% effective. Manage, Control and Report are KEYWORDS! MO is like the hearth of the department. Therefore I was responsible of the production of the KPIs, annual budget, forecast and activity reports. Finally I participated at lot of confidential projects in the department.

As Business Analyst, my second head role. First I was the business partner of the department inside the company where I was constantly in touch with the finance, risk management, sales and IT teams. I was in each cross department projects, the largest one being the transfert of the SnOP toward the Integrated Business Planning (IBP) for the Supply Chain process. I was enlist 2 days/week during 5 months and gather 20 project members, 1 audit firm, and all the ManCo involved. Then more as Analyst, a big part of my time was to worked on the hedging policy and strategy by integrating the margin securing directive. Then I was responsible of the daily hedging financial operations plus reporting of the department. Naturally I was the representative for IT service providers.

As Full Project Holder, even Project Founder, my final part. I created from A to Z, alone, the upgrading of the raw material physical purchasing architecture/process. It was my most important and challenging project in Tereos, first because it came out of my head :) and then because its involve the changement, in the way of working and thinking. The solution is an IT tool developped on Visual Basic for Application (VBA) Excel. The tool need to be automatic, intuitive and almost no hand task (for the person who work on it every day), robust and secure, sustainable and interoperable. The lenght of the project was a little more than one year, have involved 15 peoples and 4 departments. Today the tool is implemented over 8 european plants with very good customer satisfactions. Always on VBA and linked to this solution, I developed 2 others automatic tools : a control system to oversee everything is ok and a real time dashbord.
